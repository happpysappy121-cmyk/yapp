How to Reach Uttarakhand

By Air

Major airport: **Jolly Grant Airport (DED)**, Dehradun. Connects to major Indian cities.

By Rail

Main railheads are **Haridwar, Dehradun, and Kathgodam**. Excellent connectivity from Delhi.

By Road

National Highways (NH) ensure smooth travel. Regular state and private buses are available.
